Hi, I'm Christine Chye.

Santa Clara University Class of 2021
B.S Computer Science and Engineering

I love making apps and learning new technologies. One of my passions is computing for social benefit! I have done research at Santa Clara University to develop an educational app to debunk misconceptions in health care by using card games. I also have experience with React Native, JavaScript, iOS and Android development, and C/C++ programming. My other projects include HappyBoba, an iOS app, and Cards for Humanity, a website for a hackathon project.


I chose to major in Computer Science and Engineering because I wanted to learn how our everyday devices were developed and how it can be used to better society. For me, it's fun to learn how different programming languages and software come together to produce a product. I hope to pursue a graduate degree in Human-Computer Interaction and Design to further my knowledge in the insection between computer science and user experience.
