Hi, I'm Christine Chye.

Santa Clara University Class of 2021
Computer Science and Engineering Major
Economics Minor

I love making apps and learning new technologies to creatively use them. One of my passions is computing for social benefit, in which I worked on an educational app to debunk misconceptions by card games. I'm interested in back-end development and have experience with React Native, JavaScript, iOS and Android development, and C programming.

I chose to major in Computer Science and Engineering because I wanted to learn how our everyday devices were developed and how it can be used to better society. For me, it's fun to learn how different programming languages and software come together to produce a product. I also minored in Economics because I find it important to understand how firms and individuals make decisions based on scarcity. I enjoy learning about models used to optimize consumption, which in turn helps us to be better consumers and producers in society.
